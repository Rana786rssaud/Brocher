SisterGo

A women-first ride booking platform built on safety, trust and innovation.

Introduction

SisterGo is not just a ride-booking app. It is a safety-focused mobility ecosystem designed especially for women. Our platform ensures that every ride is verified, trackable, and controlled by the customer.

Our Vision

To become India’s most trusted women-centric mobility platform, enabling freedom of movement without fear, anytime and anywhere.

How SisterGo Works

01

Login & Verify

User logs in with verified identity and secure authentication.

02

04

Live Tracking & Safety

Innovative Features

Trusted Zone System

Certain areas are marked as high-safety zones with priority drivers and faster response.

Ride Guardian

AI-based monitoring detects unusual stops or route deviation and triggers alerts automatically.

Emergency Ride Switch

User can instantly change destination to nearest police station or safe location.

Women Driver Empowerment

Special onboarding and incentives for women drivers to grow employment.
